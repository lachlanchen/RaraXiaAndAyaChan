# About RaraXiaAndAyaChan

`RaraXiaAndAyaChan` is a multilingual creative repository for the Rara Xia, Aya Chan, and Sasa Kun animated story series by Lachlan Chen.

The repository shares:

- connected episode stories;
- short-form video prompts;
- Xiaoyunque / Seedance production notes;
- reusable local scripts for video preparation;
- multilingual README pages for global readers.

Public publishing policy:

- Share story drafts, prompts, documentation, and reusable scripts.
- Do not publish `.env`, access keys, Chrome profiles, raw generated videos, or private automation state.
- Keep bulky render outputs in local folders or external storage.

## Series Identity

Rara Xia is a kind but overconfident panda hero. Aya Chan is a sharp, warm red panda who catches every contradiction. Sasa Kun is a curious boy whose strange ideas make the plot move. Together they turn science, travel, food, and magic into short cinematic stories.

The main visual world is cute, cozy, bright, and emotionally funny. Even when the team flies over a black hole or fights cosmic enemies, the story should keep warmth, curiosity, and humor.
