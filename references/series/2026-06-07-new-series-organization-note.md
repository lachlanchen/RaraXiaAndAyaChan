# New Series Organization Note

Date: 2026-06-07

The story library had grown in two directions:

1. planned continuity, such as `Red Sand Paper Kite`;
2. daily customized stories that were useful but not always part of that canon.

This note records the new organization decision.

## Decision

Keep old story markdown files in place to avoid breaking prompt and workflow
links. Add a cleaner `references/series/` layer for reusable story bibles,
assets, and canon rules.

## New Series

### Chip Factory Adventures

Educational comedy series about chip manufacturing and computer architecture.
Topics include NVIDIA-style AI accelerators, Apple silicon, Foxconn-style smart
manufacturing, MediaTek NPUs, Arm, RISC-V, wafers, yield, masks, packaging,
memory, and AI factories.

### Robot, Drone, And AR Lab

Educational comedy series about robotics, drones, dexterous hands, AR glasses,
spatial computing, embodied AI, perception, SLAM, haptics, latency, and safety.

The words card becomes a physical in-scene desktop learning widget. It can show
English, Japanese, furigana, and optionally a third language, but it must be a
prop, not ordinary video subtitles.

## Future Rule

Before writing a daily story, decide whether it is:

- a standalone daily custom story;
- a red-sand continuation;
- a chip education episode;
- a robot/drone/AR education episode;
- or a new series that deserves its own folder.
