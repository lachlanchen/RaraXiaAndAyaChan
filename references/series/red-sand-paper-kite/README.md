## Core Idea

The trio follows a glowing paper kite through a red-sand world full of bronze
faces, gold birds, lamps, gates, bridges, and strange markets. The world is not
asking to be conquered. It is testing whether visitors can repair, listen, and
share roads with the weakest travelers.

## Story Tone

- Warm, mysterious, cute, and lightly epic.
- Funny first, moral second.
- Never turn the story into a lecture.
- Every episode should have a small act of care.

## Recurring Props

- LightMind AI glasses.
- Aya Chan's handmade notebook.
- Sasa Kun's bamboo.
- Glowing paper kite.
- Bronze face / gold bird / old lamp / red gate.

## Canon Rule

When a choice appears, do not choose the fastest, strongest, or brightest path
unless it also protects the smallest traveler.
